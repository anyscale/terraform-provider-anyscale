release-note:fixed
resource/anyscale_project: the delete-time 403 retry for a recently-created project (introduced in the previous release) now waits up to 60 seconds using a capped-exponential backoff instead of a fixed 3 attempts over about 6 seconds, since observed lag before the delete-time permission grant becomes visible can exceed 6 seconds; a project older than 5 minutes is still never retried.

release-note:fixed
